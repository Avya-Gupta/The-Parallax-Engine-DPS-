Services


We measure our success by how much money our clients make and that is why we offer a platform for every kind of investor. Our investment advisory extends across all major asset classes like:

Equity

An equity investment is money that is invested in a company by purchasing shares of that company in the stock market. These shares are typically traded on stock exchange.

Derivatives

They are secondary securities whose value is solely based on the value of the primary security and include forwards, futures, options, swaps and warrants.

Currencies

Trading in currencies is done through currency market, also called forex market or foreign exchange market. It helps investors take positions on different currencies.

Mutual Funds

It is a type of a financial vehicle made up of a pool of money collected by many investors to invest in securities like stocks, bonds, money market instruments and other assets.

Gold ETFs

It is an exchange traded fund (ETF) that aims to track the domestic physical gold price. They are passive investment instruments that are based on Gold prices and invest in gold bullion.

IPOs

An Initial Public Offering (IPO) refers to the process of offering shares of a private corporation to the public in a new stock issuance. It allows a company to raise capital from the market.

Frequently Asked Questions


Is Portfolio Management Service different from a mutual fund?
Portfolio management, unlike mutual funds, is a highly personalised service. Each investor has a separate portfolio of shares. Besides, there is no entry or exit load in PMS.

Is my capital guaranteed?
Equity investments are subject to market risks. Hence, the capital is not guaranteed.

Is there any lock-in period for my investments?
As per SEBI guidelines, there is no lock-in period for your investments. You can withdraw your funds partially or terminate the account at any time.

How to avail Portfolio Management Service (PMS)?
A PMS agreement has to be signed between the investor and the portfolio manager. An identity proof (copy of PAN card/Passport) along with a photograph is required.

What about my tax liability?
In PMS there are 2 types of incomes:
Short term gains: Gains realized on sale of investments within one year; taxed at 15%.
Long term gains: Gains realized on sale of investments after one year and above Rs.1 Lakh; taxed at 10%.


I’m an NRI. Can I avail of your Portfolio Management Service (PMS)?
Yes you can, through the Portfolio Investment Scheme (as allowed by the Reserve Bank of India) either on a repatriation or non-repatriation basis.

How can I track my investments?
We update you with information in the following ways:

  • Daily reporting by e-mail/ Weekly hard copy option
  • Complete picture on your investment though our website
  • Audited, comprehensive year-end report to facilitate tax payments
  • Access to relationship manager any time
  • Access to short notes generated by our in-house team
